The Board of Professional Digital Artists will conduct comprehensive evaluations and assign ratings to NFTs, taking into account a multitude of criteria.                                                       

The NFTrating community engages in a democratic voting process to appraise listed NFTs, with considerations given to design aesthetics, the nuance of artistry, originality, and other pertinent attributes.                                                       

The NFTrating platform initiates a series of digital design contests on a monthly basis, aimed at showcasing and nurturing exceptional talent among NFT creators worldwide.                                                       

Our platform curates a comprehensive directory of purchase and sale opportunities for NFTs, sourced from a diverse array of marketplaces, thereby enhancing accessibility to our robust customer base and community support network.

The NFTrating chat lobby is meticulously designed to serve as a nexus for premier NFT creators and aficionados, facilitating direct and meaningful interactions within the community.

Engage in a diverse array of remunerative programs, such as casting votes for esteemed NFT creators, disseminating referral links, and listing your own NFTs, to accrue our exclusive NFTR token incentives.

Rankings are conferred based on a composite of evaluations from our esteemed panel of professional digital artists and the collective voting of our community. The highest accolade, denoted as ‘X’, signifies the pinnacle of achievement with the 1st rank. The ‘Y’ designation represents a commendable performance, meriting the 2nd rank, while the ‘Z’ category acknowledges burgeoning talent with the 3rd rank.
